What is Python?


Python is a powerful, easy-to-learn programming language known for its simplicity and versatility. Created by Guido van Rossum and released in 1991, Python has grown to become one of the most widely used languages in the world due to its clear syntax and vast ecosystem of libraries.


Python has played a crucial role in the rise of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. Its rich frameworks like TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Scikit-learn have empowered developers and researchers to build advanced AI models with ease. From automating simple tasks to solving complex problems across industries, Python continues to drive innovation and open doors to limitless opportunities in the AI-driven world.
