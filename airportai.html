AI Experience in Airport Systems


As a Senior Engineering Manager, I have spearheaded the integration of AI technologies into airport operations, driving digital transformation across safety, security, and passenger experience. My expertise covers a wide range of AI-powered applications, including facial recognition, video analytics, and intelligent automation — all tailored to meet the rigorous demands of airport environments.


1. Facial Recognition Access Control

Implemented secure and frictionless facial recognition-based access systems for airport staff and contractors. Key features:


  • AI-based biometric verification entry for Staffs at access gates.
  • Central logging of access events for audits and compliance.

2. Facial Recognition for Wanted Individuals

Deployed AI-driven facial recognition surveillance to identify and flag individuals on security or criminal watchlists.


  • Real-time alerts to airport security command centers.
  • Privacy-aware, GDPR-compliant architecture.

3. Number Plate Recognition (ANPR) & Vehicle Watchlist Matching

Advanced number plate recognition systems installed at entry/exit points, parking lots, and critical zones.


  • Real-time detection of blacklisted vehicles.
  • Automated barrier control for authorized vehicles.
  • Incident reporting and alerting to airport security.

4. Fly to Gate – Seamless Passenger Journey

Designed and supported Fly to Gate solutions that allow passengers to move through the entire airport journey using just their face as their boarding pass.


Components include:

  • Immigration and security checkpoints using facial authentication.
  • Biometric boarding gates eliminating the need for paper boarding passes.

Benefits:

  • Reduced queue times and operational costs.
  • Enhanced security through verified identity at each touchpoint.
  • Improved passenger satisfaction with a frictionless experience.

5. Video Analytics for Airport Scenarios

I have deployed AI-based video analytics across various zones in the airport — from terminals to airside areas — to enable real-time monitoring, incident detection, and operational efficiency. These systems help prevent security incidents, enhance passenger experience, and support compliance with safety regulations.


Key solutions include:

  • Aircraft Turnaround Monitoring

    • AI-powered analysis of ground operations to track and optimize tasks such as fueling, catering, baggage handling, and cleaning to minimize aircraft ground time.

  • Without Vest Detection

    • Detects staff operating in restricted or high-risk zones without high-visibility vests, triggering immediate safety alerts.

  • Passenger Queue Monitoring

    • Analyzes queue lengths and wait times at check-in, immigration, and security checkpoints using overhead camera feeds. Data is visualized on live dashboards to improve staffing and reduce bottlenecks.

  • Abandoned Baggage Detection

    • Uses motion and object recognition to flag unattended luggage in terminals, with alert systems linked to security protocols.

  • Loitering and Trespassing Alerts

    • Identifies suspicious behavior or unauthorized access in restricted or sensitive airport zones through pattern recognition and movement tracking.

  • Runway and Taxiway Intrusion Detection

    • Detects unauthorized movement (people, animals, or vehicles) on active runways or taxiways, improving airside safety and regulatory compliance.

  • Slip & Fall Detection

    • AI models identify fall incidents in real time, enabling immediate intervention and assisting in incident documentation for liability and safety reviews.

  • Smoke and Fire Detection via Video

    • Vision-based detection of smoke and fire in areas not covered by traditional sensors, providing earlier warnings in critical areas like baggage handling or maintenance zones.

  • Crowd Detection and Density Monitoring

    • Uses overhead and wide-angle cameras to measure crowd density in real time, particularly during peak hours or emergency situations.

    • Prevents overcrowding in boarding gates, immigration areas, and duty-free zones.
    • Enables dynamic space reallocation and proactive deployment of airport staff.
    • Supports compliance with health and safety standards, especially during public health events (e.g., COVID-19).
