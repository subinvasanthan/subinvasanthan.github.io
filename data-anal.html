Data Analytics Experience in Airport Operations


In my role as a data analytics professional, I have worked extensively on systems that support digital transformation in airport environments. My experience involves turning complex, multi-source operational data into actionable insights that drive efficiency, improve passenger experiences, and support real-time decision-making.


Key Highlights:


  • Real-Time Data Integration:

    Hands-on experience in designing data pipelines that ingest and process live data from a wide range of airport subsystems, including flight information displays, passenger tracking, baggage systems, and terminal operations. Real-time ingestion was managed using Apache Kafka to ensure low-latency, high-throughput data delivery.


  • Data Warehousing & Storage Architecture:

    Worked with high-performance analytics databases like ClickHouse for large-scale data analysis and MariaDB for transactional and reference data, enabling a hybrid approach to data storage that supports both real-time insights and historical trend analysis.


  • Interactive Dashboards & Visualizations:
